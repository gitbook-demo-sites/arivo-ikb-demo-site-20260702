## Company context

Arivo helps simplify auto lending for customers and dealers. The public site highlights Arivo One for customer payments, autopay, transaction history, and support, plus dealer-facing PinPoint experiences for desking and structuring.

This internal knowledge base concept extends that simplicity to employees: policies become searchable, structured, governed, and easier to update than long shared documents.

{% columns %}
{% column width="50%" %}
### For servicing teams

Find current policy guidance for account statuses, payments, customer communication, disclosures, skip tracing, and door knock workflows.
{% endcolumn %}

{% column width="50%" %}
### For policy owners

Maintain review dates, approval metadata, update notes, and reusable procedure blocks across a growing policy library.
{% endcolumn %}
{% endcolumns %}

## Suggested searches

- What does the policy say about active account status?
- How does the payment waterfall work?
- Which customer communication rules need compliance review?
- What policy pages are stale or missing an approval date?
